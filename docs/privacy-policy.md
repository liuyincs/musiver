# Privacy Policy

We highly value user privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using Musiver.

## Information Collection and Use

### 1. Information You Provide

- You sign in with the account of your self-hosted music library.
- Account credentials are stored locally on your device.
- If you choose to activate membership, we may collect your email address to restore membership status across devices.
- Email-based restoration is optional. You can still restore membership using your order number.

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

When you use the app, we may automatically collect:

- Device information, including device model and operating system version.
- Log information, including app usage, crash logs, and error reports.

## Purposes of Processing

We use collected information to:

- Provide core player features and services, including playback, management, and browsing of your music library.
- Improve app performance and user experience.
- Handle technical support and user feedback.
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations.

## Information Protection

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. This includes encryption during transmission and secure storage practices.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following situations:

- Disclosure required by applicable laws, court orders, or government authorities.
- Sharing necessary information with trusted service providers to deliver app functionality and services.
- Disclosure reasonably necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety.

## Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We do not control their privacy practices and recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

## Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will provide prominent notice through appropriate channels.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact our support team.

This policy applies only to Musiver and does not apply to third-party apps or services, even when accessed through Musiver.
